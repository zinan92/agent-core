# Skill-Driven Organization

## Claim

A company can be modeled as a finite bundle of roles, SOPs, skills, and steps rather than as a vague collection of employees.

## Why It Matters

- it makes onboarding reproducible
- it makes delegation more precise
- it separates durable operating preference from temporary human execution

## Practical Rule

When defining a role, first identify its core SOP bundle.

Only then decide which skills and runtime environment the role needs.
